About Us

This sample video is provided solely for demonstration purposes and is not affiliated, associated, endorsed, or connected in any way with Rex Hotel.

Northern Accom with Travel and Tours specializes in providing comfortable, centrally located accommodations and memorable tour experiences around Baguio City. Situated near the bustling Session Road and the popular SM City Baguio, their accommodations make it easy for guests to explore local attractions, vibrant markets, and cultural sites. Their tour offerings cover a range of must-see destinations, giving visitors insights into Baguio’s rich history, scenic views, and cool mountain atmosphere. Known for its commitment to guest satisfaction, Northern Accom creates a welcoming, hassle-free travel experience in one of the Philippines' most beloved cities.
